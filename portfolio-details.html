Banking app

  • Rating:
  • Client: personal

This app solved two potential problems in banking apps here in Nigeria. Map and transfer function. Many users on many occasions mistakenly transfer funds to the wrong recipient because they probably mixed up one or two of the recipients account number, the app shows the profile containing the full name and picture of the account number provided for funds transfer thereby getting rid of the problem. The app also includes a map that directs users to the banks closest branch.
